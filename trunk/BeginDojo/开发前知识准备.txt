1. 理解Dojo库JavaScript类, 包的概念
    面对JavaScript, 你会说"我连定义一个类的语法都不知道"
    由于JavaScript没有class的概念, 第3方库对其扩展包装出class概念.
    参考:
    Understanding dojo.declare, dojo.require, and dojo.provide
    http://dojocampus.org/content/2008/06/03/understanding-dojodeclare-dojorequire-and-dojoprovide/

    Dojo Module Packaging and Loading

    Configuring Dojo with djConfig
    http://dojotoolkit.org/reference-guide/djConfig.html

    Maps a module name to a path
